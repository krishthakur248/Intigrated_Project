SkillXchange connects people who want to learn with people who want to teach through a simple, secure platform. Here's how to get started and make the most of your experience.

1

Create Your Profile

Sign up for a free account and build your profile. List the skills you can teach and those you'd like to learn. The more detailed your profile, the better your matches will be.

2

Find Skill Matches

Our algorithm will suggest potential matches based on complementary skills. You can also browse the community manually to find specific skills or people with similar interests.

3

Propose an Exchange

When you find someone with a skill you want to learn (who might also want to learn something from you), send them an exchange proposal. Define what you want to learn and what you can teach in return.

4

Plan Your Sessions

Once your proposal is accepted, use our messaging system to plan your exchange sessions. You can meet virtually through our integrated video platform or arrange in-person meetings if you're in the same area.

5

Exchange Skills

Conduct your teaching and learning sessions according to your agreement. Whether it's one session or a series of lessons, the exchange should be balanced and beneficial for both parties.

6

Rate & Review

After the exchange, leave a review for your partner to help build trust in the community. Share what you learned and how the experience went.

Exchange Models

SkillXchange offers different ways to exchange skills:

Direct Exchange

You teach something to the same person who teaches you something in return. For example, you teach photography while they teach you cooking.

Community Exchange

You teach Person A, Person A teaches Person B, and Person B teaches you. This creates a community chain of skill sharing.

Credit System

You earn credits by teaching and spend them to learn from others. This works well when you don't have a direct skill match with someone.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is SkillXchange completely free?

The basic platform is free to use. We offer premium features for a small subscription fee, but all core functionality is available to everyone.

How do I stay safe when meeting in person?

We recommend first meetings in public places, bringing a friend if possible, and using our in-app verification system to confirm identities.

What if the exchange isn't working out?

You can end an exchange at any time. If there are issues, our support team can help mediate or find solutions.

Can I teach multiple people at once?

Yes! Group sessions are encouraged for certain skills. Our platform can help you organize and manage group exchanges.
